Vicsek + Evolutionary Game + Loner

See how a small swarm evolves instead of only reading aggregate curves.

This interactive demo visualizes ten agents moving in a periodic box, updating headings through local alignment, and switching strategies through a Fermi-style imitation rule with loners.

Strategies

C: cooperator, aligns and pays communication cost

D: defector, moves with random heading

L: loner, aligns but imitates with factor p

Rule summary

Neighbors are radius-based under periodic boundary conditions.

C and L follow local average direction plus noise. D randomizes.

Payoff is local order minus alpha-scaled communication cost.
